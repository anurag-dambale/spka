About Us

SPKA & Associates, Chartered Accountants, is a firm engaged in providing wide range of services such as Audit & Assurance, Direct & Indirect Taxes, Regulatory and Advisory Services to various sectors for the last 2 years. The firm has it's head office at Chhatrapati Sambahjinagar and two branch offices, one is at Pune & another is at Ahmednagar. Our team represents a combination of skilled professionals which are geared to offer best & high quality services.
